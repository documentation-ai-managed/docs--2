---
title: AI Assistant Not Loading
description: Fix issues when the AI assistant fails to appear in 春雪 马.
---

## Symptoms

- AI assistant button is missing
- "Unable to connect AI" error displays

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh your browser">
    ````
    Press Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Command+Shift+R (Mac).
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your internet connection">
    Ensure connectivity for Spring Snow Horse features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear cookies and cache">
    Log in again if prompted.
  </Step>
</Steps>
