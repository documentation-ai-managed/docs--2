## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Projects">
    Open your "春雪 马" project dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Article'">
    Enter a title and summary for your documentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use AI Editor">
    ````
    Type a prompt or select "Generate Section" for instant content.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Save and click "Publish". Your docs are now live!
  </Step>
</Steps>
