## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

- A modern web browser like Chrome or Firefox
- An email address for account verification
- Access to your team's preferred workspace name

<Callout kind="info">
  No credit card required for the free tier. Upgrade anytime from your dashboard.
</Callout>

## Create and Verify Your Account

Sign up at `https://app.documentation.ai/register` to get started immediately.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Navigate to the registration page and enter your email, password, and name.

    Choose your plan (start with Free for testing).

  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification email from Documentation.AI.

    Click the link to activate your account. Verification takes `<30s`.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to `https://app.documentation.ai/login` and sign in with your credentials.

    You'll land on the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Configure Workspace Settings

Set up your first workspace to organize projects.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Basic Setup" icon="settings">
    From the dashboard, click **New Workspace**.

    Enter a name like `my-team-docs` and select your timezone.

    <CodeGroup tabs="JSON,YAML">
    ````json
    {
      "workspace": "my-team-docs",
      "timezone": "UTC",
      "theme": "light"
    }
    ````

    ````yaml
    workspace: my-team-docs
    timezone: UTC
    theme: light
    ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Advanced" icon="sliders">
    Enable AI assistant and set default language to English.

    Save changes to apply.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Workspaces isolate projects. Create one per team or product.
</Callout>

## Build Your First Documentation Site

Launch a new site with pre-built templates.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Site" icon="file-plus">
    In your workspace, select **New Site** > **Blank Template**.

    Name it `Quickstart Guide` and choose MDX format.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Content" icon="edit-3">
    Use the editor to add a frontmatter and H2 headings.

    Preview changes in real-time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="globe">
    Click **Publish** to deploy at `https://docs.documentation.ai/quickstart-guide`.

    Share the live URL with your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Access the AI Assistant

Speed up edits with intelligent assistance.

1. Open your site editor.
2. Click the AI icon in the toolbar.
3. Type prompts like "Add a steps component for installation".

The AI generates MDX code you can tweak and insert.

<CodeGroup tabs="Prompt,Generated">
````bash
# Example Prompt
Generate a Steps component for npm install process.
````

````jsx
<Steps>
  <Step title="Install">npm install documentation-ai-sdk</Step>
</Steps>
````
</CodeGroup>
